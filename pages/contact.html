Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we often respond within 2-4 hours.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your contact information is only used to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing feature suggestions from our community. Use the "Feature Request" subject when contacting us, and we'll review all suggestions carefully.

How do I report a bug?

Please select "Bug Report" as your subject and include as much detail as possible: what you were doing, what browser you're using, and what happened vs. what you expected.

Do you offer educational partnerships?

Yes! We work with schools, libraries, and educational organizations. Contact us with "Partnership Inquiry" to discuss how we can collaborate.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the "Content Concern" subject and provide the specific content link or description. We review all reports and take appropriate action quickly.
