Learn About Bias

Confirmation Bias

The tendency to search for information that confirms our existing beliefs.

Selection Bias

Cherry-picking facts or quotes that support a particular narrative.

Sensationalism

Using shocking or emotional language to grab attention.

Bias Detection Checklist

Check the source's reputation
Look for emotional language
Verify facts independently
Check for multiple perspectives
Examine the evidence provided
Consider what's not mentioned
