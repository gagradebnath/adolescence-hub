Scarcity Principle

Creates artificial scarcity to pressure quick decisions

Authority Bias

Uses fake expert endorsements to appear credible

Examples found: "As seen on TV", unverified doctor quotes
How to Protect Yourself
Before Buying
  • Wait 24 hours before making impulse purchases
  • Research the product independently
  • Check for verified reviews on multiple platforms
  • Reverse-search product images for dropshipping
Red Flags to Watch
  • Too-good-to-be-true claims
  • Extreme urgency or pressure tactics
  • Lack of clear return/refund policy
  • No contact information or company details
Smart Shopping
  • Use price comparison tools
  • Check Better Business Bureau ratings
  • Look for SSL certificates and secure payment
  • Read the fine print and terms of service
